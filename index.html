    about

    "This is the one place I can truly be myself."

    Welcome to The Reading List, the only fully nocturnal book club for vampires.

    We are the only book club to boast:

    • Background-checked non-slayer members
    • Comfy coffins in the break rooms
    • Delicious human snacks

    meeting info

    We meet once a month at the full moon at 1.30am (except for blue moons!)

    We meet on a broody bridge at dusk in some ambiguously European city (Charles Bridge, Prague)

    Check out our group pictures! Unfortunately none of us show up on camera but we thought we'd memorialise the occasion.
